Our Story

Nestled along the pristine coastline, Luna Vista Resort has been welcoming guests for over two decades. Our family-owned property combines modern luxury with timeless tropical charm, offering an unforgettable escape from the everyday.

What began as a small beachfront retreat has grown into a premier destination, renowned for our personalized service and breathtaking ocean views.

Our Vision

At Luna Vista, we believe in sustainable luxury. We preserve our natural surroundings while providing world-class amenities. Every detail - from our locally sourced cuisine to our eco-friendly practices - reflects our commitment to both our guests and our environment.
